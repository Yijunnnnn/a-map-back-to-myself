Title: Studio Journal: Recovering a Print
Date: 2026-06-27
Source: mira_027_studio_journal.md
Demo Persona: Mira Vale
Privacy Level: private

One of my linocut prints smeared during the ink transfer. Instead of throwing it away, I cut out the clean portions and used them as elements in a mixed-media collage. The resulting piece has a unique, layered character that I couldn't have planned.
